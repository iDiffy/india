Attractions

  • Taj Mahal

    The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the Yamuna river in the Indian city of Agra.

  • Delhi

    Delhi officially the National Capital Territory of Delhi, is a city and a union territory of India containing New Delhi, the capital of India.

  • Mumbai

    Mumbai is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra and the de facto financial centre of India.

  • Kolkata

    Kolkata is the capital of the Indian state of West Bengal, on the eastern bank of the Hooghly River west of the border with Bangladesh.

  • Bengaluru

    Bangaluru is the capital and largest city of the southern Indian state of Karnataka.
